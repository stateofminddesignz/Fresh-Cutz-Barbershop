About

Welcome to Fresh Cutz! We are a black-owned and operated business located in the heart of Gainesville, Florida. Our team of skilled barbers is dedicated to providing top-notch haircuts, shaves, and grooming services to our valued clients. At our shop, we pride ourselves on creating a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere for all. Whether you're looking for a fresh cut or a relaxing shave, we have the expertise and equipment to help you look and feel your best. In addition to our regular services, we also offer a range of specialized treatments and styling options to suit your unique needs and preferences. From fades and tapers to beard trims and hot towel shaves, we have something for everyone. We are passionate about supporting and uplifting our community, and we are committed to providing high-quality services and products that reflect our values. Thank you for choosing our barber shop, and we look forward to serving you soon!
